Week 3 (Sample)

Monday

Today I participated in the repair of a worn-out roller shaft. I inspected the damaged part, cleaned the surface thoroughly, prepared it for welding, rebuilt the worn area using arc welding, ground the repaired surface to restore the profile, and carried out a visual inspection for weld quality. This activity strengthened my practical understanding of repair welding, surface preparation, quality inspection, and safe use of fabrication equipment.
